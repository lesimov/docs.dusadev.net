## Perks & Bonuses

### Passive Perks (Auto-Active)

**Level-Based:**
- XP multipliers (increases with level)
- Quality chance bonuses
- Shop discounts
- Extended trap durations

**Achievement-Based:**
- Hunt 100 animals: +5% XP
- 50 Perfect kills: +10% quality chance
- Complete 25 quests: +15% quest rewards
- Win 5 tournaments: +20% tournament points

### Active Abilities

Unlock special abilities as you level:

**Eagle Eye (Level 10)**
- Highlight animals through vegetation
- See animal quality before kill
- Cooldown: 5 minutes
- Duration: 30 seconds

**Silent Step (Level 15)**
- Move without scaring animals
- Perfect for bow hunting
- Cooldown: 10 minutes
- Duration: 2 minutes

**Master Tracker (Level 20)**
- See animal tracks and paths
- Predict spawn locations
- Passive ability (always on)

## Leveling Strategies

### Fast Leveling (1-10)

**Focus:**
- Hunt common animals (Rabbit, Deer)
- Complete all daily quests
- Use XP boost perks
- Hunt in groups (if XP shared)

**Route:**
1. Accept daily quests
2. Hunt in Paleto Forest
3. Focus on quest objectives
4. Use perfect quality for bonus XP
5. Repeat daily

**Expected Time:** 5-10 hours

### Efficient Leveling (11-20)

**Focus:**
- Weekly quests (high XP)
- Rare animal hunting
- Tournament participation
- Perfect quality kills only

**Route:**
1. Complete weekly quest (1,000+ XP)
2. Hunt rare animals in premium zones
3. Participate in both weekend tournaments
4. Maintain perfect kill streak
5. Cook all meat for extra XP

**Expected Time:** 15-25 hours
