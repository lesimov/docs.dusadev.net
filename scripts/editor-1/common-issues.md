<details>

<summary>How can I prevent gloves to leave fingerprint on vehicle?</summary>

1. Find your glove number from your clothing menu
2. Go to dusa\_evidence/configurations/config.lua
3. Find WhitelistedGloves on config
4. Add that number to that option
